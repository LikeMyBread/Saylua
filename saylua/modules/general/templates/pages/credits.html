Saylua Credits

Saylua was primarily created by Mike and Tiff though the project would not have been possible without the contributions of many individuals.

Most of the art for Saylua, including the pet art, was wonderfully drawn by Chutkat who also designed the overall visual style for the site.

The world of Saylua was brought to life through the beautiful environment illustrations of Lyraina.

Many additional artists including Fyne, Synthaphonograph, Cheetahtrout, and Fasius contributed many important pieces of art early on in the project's life.

Much of Saylua's lore was expanded upon through writing contributions from ArtsyAxolotl.

And a special mention to the early members of the project who helped significantly shape the direction of Saylua with their feedback, ideas, and encouragement: Namine, Gam, Tears, Inu, Vik, Jamew, and Queenie.
