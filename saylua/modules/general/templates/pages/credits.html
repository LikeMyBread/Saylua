Saylua Credits

Although Saylua was primarily created by Mike and Tiff, the game would not have been possible without the contributions of many individuals.

Most of the art for Saylua, including the pet art, was wonderfully drawn by Chutkat who also designed the overall visual style for the site.

The world of Saylua was brought to life through the beautiful environment illustrations of Lyraina.

The logo for Saylua was created by IrianWhitefox.

Many early programming contributions to the project were created by Noi Sek who particularly helped with getting our Adventure feature off the ground.

Several additional artists including Fyne, Synthaphonograph, Cheetahtrout, Ravna-Resta, and Fasius contributed artwork early on in the project's life.

Much of Saylua's lore was expanded upon through writing contributions from ArtsyAxolotl.

And a special thanks to the early members of the project who helped shape the direction of Saylua with their feedback, ideas, and encouragement: Namine, Gam, Tears, Inu, Vik, and Queenie.
