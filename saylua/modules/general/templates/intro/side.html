Starting Your Journey

It was time for you to head out for the next chapter of your life. You were ready to move closer to the countryside, a place where you could be closer to nature.

The planet of Saylua is divided into two main sides, a dark side and a light side. Both sides have their own draws, and you can choose to live in either.

Which side will you choose?

Sayleus, the light side

In Sayleus, vivid meadows often transition into deep woods where the light shines brightly through the trees. The light side is home to many large, thriving plants and elegant creatures.

Luaria, the dark side

Luaria is covered in a variety of magical plants and creatures. Although the side is engulfed by an endless darkness, the presence of high amounts of magical energy has allowed Luaria to prosper in its own ways.

Of course, you shouldn't worry too much if you regret your decision down the road. It will always be possible for you to move again.
